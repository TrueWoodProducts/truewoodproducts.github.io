True Wood – Natural Handmade Wooden Products

Every True Wood board is one of a kind. We don’t use conveyor belts, templates, or mass production. Everything begins with a single piece of hardwood — oak, walnut, or ash. We carefully select each cut, respecting the grain, color, and natural texture.

No glue. No chemicals. No shortcuts. Only wood, oil, and the touch of a craftsman’s hand. Every board is crafted individually, infused with warmth and story. Walnut, in particular, is our pride — each piece is as unique as a fingerprint, never to be repeated.

At True Wood, we believe in creating pieces that are not just tools, but companions in your kitchen — full of life and meaning.

How to Care for Your True Wood Board

  • Hand wash only. Use warm water, a soft sponge, and a drop of mild soap if needed.
  • Never soak or put in a dishwasher.
  • Dry upright and avoid drying near heat sources.
  • Oil regularly — once a month with food-safe oils like linseed, coconut, almond, or board conditioner.
  • Store in a dry place, away from direct sunlight or heat.
